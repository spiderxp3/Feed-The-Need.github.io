Usage and Functionality

These activities will be common for all three roles
  • Volunteers
  • Individual Donors
  • Food Chains
Advertisement for Individual Donations

The goal of this activity is to bring to their attention all the donations that are available near them and should be donated as soon as possible.

Booking of Meal

Volunteers need to book meals by specifying the number of meals they require and the pickup time. Volunteers must always order fewer meals than the total available.

Status of Meal

This activity will specify the status of the meal from the time of Pick Up until it is verified. Four statuses include-
Booked, Picked, Verified,Cancelled

Quest Activity

This is an initiative raised by volunteers for extra-social activities that involve educating children, distributing items, or bringing awareness to social issues.

Lead Volunteer Activity

When a volunteer posts a quest, details such as its location, goal, and date will appear in quest activity. A volunteer can raise only one quest at a time.

Participating Volunteer

If volunteers do not raise a quest, the advertisement for other quests will be displayed here as a card view, and volunteers can choose to participate in any quest.

Donation Status

Donors need to provide a description, a title, and an image of the item they are donating along with a suitable pick-up time and they will be able to see multiple details.

Add

Donors have the option to oversee donations made by them via status - Add

Booked

Donor shall view the items booked by him and her.

Posted

Donor will be able to see history of bookings and status.

Distributed

Donors can oversee distributed donations made by them via status - Distributed

Meal Information

Food chains are supposed to add descriptions and images of meals they wish to provide. Booking meals through POS is only possible once the meal details have been added.

Point of Sale

The interface allows the public to order a meal for the needy from the food chain. The number of meals they can book is not limited; additionally, their name and contact information is required for records.

Status of Donation

As a way of ensuring donations reach the right people, restaurant/food chains will keep a record of volunteers in the form of card views and information until the donation is completely verified.

Easy Reading

Reading focus mode for long form articles, ebooks and other materials which involve large text areas

User Interface

To make the process of food donation more transparent, we designed POS so that donors can see the description of meals they are donating at food chains. .
